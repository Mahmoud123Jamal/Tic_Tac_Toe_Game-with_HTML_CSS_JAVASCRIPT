About Tic Tac Toe :
    Tic-tac-toe is a game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid. 
    The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game.
    Noughts and Crosses :
    Tic Tac Toe is known by a few other names around the world. The most common of these is 
    "Noughts and Crosses" or simply the "XO game."
    Tic Tac Toe History :
    Did you know that variations of Tic Tac Toe have been played since the Roman Empire and 3 
    in a row games can be traced all the way back to ancient Egypt!
